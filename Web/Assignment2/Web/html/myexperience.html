My Experience with Covid-19 has been one of mixed feelings.For me Covid-19 gave me a break from school which was needed in my life. When I first heard of the quarantine I didn't mind, my life was alright I would say happy ,relaxing and enjoying myself. As time goes by things started to fade as people were dying as if it was spring season and leaves were falling from trees.Even though many of these deaths did not took place in my country (Jamaica) it still had an impact on me. As time goes by more cases began to arise which resulted in many deaths, I wasn't happy.In this life you cannot think about self you have to think about other, I was at my house thinking about all those individuals that lost their jobs due to Covid-19, "How will they eat, How will they feed their families as some of those individuals are the only breadwinners".

Life is a mystery, your here today and within a second your gone.Many people die due to Covid-19 all over the world there is no cure for it but governments are trying thier best to reopen with necessary precautions so people can get back to thier lives. I'm grateful,as Covid-19 was getting to everyone. Below is a scale that shows the progess of covid 19 in my life, things were bad at first putting my life at 25% , then it got better to 50% as majority of the society was working with the governments, got even better as the number of cases began to ease in Jamaica and recoveries were increasing moving it up to 75% and now the country is about to reopen soon which I would say 85% of my life is back. What happened to the next 15% many would ask however, I did not put it at 100% / did not put in the other 15% because Covid-19 is still here, it might not be affecting my country as bad as many others however the society should always be on the look out.
